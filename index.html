Why Córdoba?

Córdoba de la Nueva Andalucía

Córdoba is the second largest city in Argentina. It was founded by Spanish conquistador, Jeronimo Luis de Cabrera, who named it Córdoba de la Nueva Andalucía, after the Spanish city with the same name, due to its similar geographic characteristics.
Córdoba is is known for its rich colonial heritage, a rich cultural offer, which includes its own unique latin pop music genre, and a vibrant nightlife with a wide choice of bars, restaurants and craft fares.

What does Cordoba offer?>

Cultural heritage

The historic center of Córdoba was built by 17th century missionaries and it comprises a full 10.000 sq2 of protected buildings which were named a World Heritage Site in 2000. Inside the vault of one os these buildings, you can enjoy a coffee at Marmol Sigo 17, a recentrly discovered and restaured section of the vault and converted into cafe.

What else can you do around this area?
